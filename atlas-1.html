The model

What you are actually shaping: Geometry of Meaning

Atlas is built on the idea that meaning is not a list. Meaning has shape.

The Geometry of Meaning is the spatial relationship between tags in the XY field. Their distances, alignments, and heat values create a structure — and images settle according to that structure.

When tags move, the geometry changes. When heat shifts, the geometry warps. When anchor images are added, the geometry bends toward them.

Atlas doesn’t just filter images. It resolves equilibrium inside a living geometric system.
